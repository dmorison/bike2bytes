Just a little bit about me

Background

Growing up in South Africa I use to ride my mountain bike a lot. When I wasn't riding my mountain bike I was either blogging about my bike racing endeavours or concentrating on my university studies. I moved to London and exchanged my racing legs for commuting legs and a career in web development, along with an extra 10kgs in body weight.

My passion for mathematics and science upon leaving school drew me into the academic world in which I went onto aquiring a Master of Science Degree from the University of Cape Town, South Africa.
